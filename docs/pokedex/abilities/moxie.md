# Moxie

## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Full Description"

    This Pokémon's Attack rises one stage upon knocking out another Pokémon, even a friendly Pokémon. This ability does not take effect when the Pokémon indirectly causes another Pokémon to faint, e.g. through poison or spikes. If this Pokémon knocks out a Pokémon with mummy, the former's ability will change without taking effect.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Raises Attack one stage upon KOing a Pokémon.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    Boosts Attack after knocking out any Pokémon.
