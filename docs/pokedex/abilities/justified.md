# Justified

## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Full Description"

    Whenever a dark-type move hits this Pokémon, its Attack rises one stage. The move is not negated in any way.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Raises Attack one stage upon taking damage from a dark move.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    Raises Attack when hit by a Dark-type move.
