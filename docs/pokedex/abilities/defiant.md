# Defiant

## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Full Description"

    When any of this Pokémon's stats are lowered, its Attack rises by two stages. If multiple stats are lowered at once, this ability takes effect with each stat lowered.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Raises Attack two stages upon having any stat lowered.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    When its stats are lowered its Attack increases.
