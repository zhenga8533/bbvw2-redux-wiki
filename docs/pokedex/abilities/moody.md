# Moody

## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Full Description"

    After each turn, one of this Pokémon's stats at random rises two stages, and another falls one stage. If a stat is already at 6 or -6 stages, it will not be chosen to be increased or decreased, respectively.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Raises a random stat two stages and lowers another one stage after each turn.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    Raises one stat and lowers another.
