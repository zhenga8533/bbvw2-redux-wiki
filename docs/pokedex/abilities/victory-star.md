# Victory Star

## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Full Description"

    All friendly Pokémon's moves, including this Pokémon's own moves, have 1.1× their usual accuracy while this Pokémon is in battle.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Increases moves' accuracy to 1.1× for friendly Pokémon.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    Boosts the accuracy of its allies and itself.
