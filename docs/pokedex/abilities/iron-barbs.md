# Iron Barbs

## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Full Description"

    Whenever a move makes contact with this Pokémon, the move's user takes 1/8 of its maximum HP in damage. This ability functions identically to rough skin.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Damages attacking Pokémon for 1/8 their max HP on contact.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    Inflicts damage to the Pokémon on contact.
