# Magic Guard

## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Full Description"

    Paralysis cannot prevent the Pokémon from moving, though the Speed cut is unaffected. Poison still damages the Pokémon outside of battle.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Protects against damage not directly caused by a move.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    The Pokémon only takes damage from attacks.
