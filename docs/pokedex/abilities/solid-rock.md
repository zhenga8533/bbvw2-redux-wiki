# Solid Rock

## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Full Description"

    This Pokémon takes 0.75× as much damage from moves that are super effective against it. This ability functions identically to filter.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Decreases damage taken from super-effective moves by 1/4.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    Reduces damage from supereffective attacks.
