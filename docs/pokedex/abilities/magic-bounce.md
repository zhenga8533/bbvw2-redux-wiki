# Magic Bounce

## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Full Description"

    When this Pokémon is targeted by a move flagged as being reflectable, the move is redirected to its user. All reflectable moves are non-damaging, and most non-damaging moves that target other Pokémon are reflectable. A move reflected by this ability or magic coat cannot be reflected back.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Reflects most non-damaging moves back at their user.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    Reflects status- changing moves.
