# Analytic

## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Full Description"

    This Pokémon's moves have 1.3× their power when it moves last in a turn. future sight and doom desire are unaffected.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Strengthens moves to 1.3× their power when moving last.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    Boosts move power when the Pokémon moves last.
