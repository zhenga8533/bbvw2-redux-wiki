!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Has a 20% chance to lower the target’s Special Defense by one stage.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Has a 20% chance to lower the target’s Special Defense by one stage.

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"
