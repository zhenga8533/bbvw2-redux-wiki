## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Has a 10% chance to raise the user’s Defense one stage.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Has a 10% chance to raise the user’s Defense by one stage.

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"
