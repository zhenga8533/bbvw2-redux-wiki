## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Raises the user’s Attack and Special Attack by one stage each. During sunny day, raises both stats by two stages.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Raises the user’s Attack and Special Attack by one stage.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    The user’s body grows all at once, raising the Attack and Sp. Atk stats.
