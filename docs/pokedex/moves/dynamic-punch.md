## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Has a 100% chance to confuse the target.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Has a 100% chance to confuse the target.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"
