## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    User pays half its max HP to raise its Attack to +6 stages. If the user cannot pay the HP cost, this move will fail.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    User pays half its max HP to max out its Attack.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    The user maximizes its Attack stat in exchange for HP equal to half its max HP.
