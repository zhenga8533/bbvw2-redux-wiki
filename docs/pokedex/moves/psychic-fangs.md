## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Destroys any light screen or reflect on the target’s side of the field, then inflicts regular damage.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Destroys Reflect and Light Screen.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    The user bites the target with its psychic capabilities. This can also destroy Light Screen and Reflect.
