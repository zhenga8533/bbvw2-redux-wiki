## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Changes the target’s ability to insomnia. If the target’s ability is truant or multitype, this move will fail.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Changes the target’s ability to Insomnia.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    A seed that causes worry is planted on the target. It prevents sleep by making its Ability Insomnia.
