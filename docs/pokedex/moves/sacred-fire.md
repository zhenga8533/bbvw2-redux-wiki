## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Has a 50% chance to burn the target. Frozen Pokémon may use this move, in which case they will thaw.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Has a 50% chance to burn the target. Lets frozen Pokémon thaw themselves.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    The target is razed with a mystical fire of great intensity. It may also leave the target with a burn.
