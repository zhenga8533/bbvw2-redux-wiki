## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Averages the user’s unmodified Defense with the target’s unmodified Defense; the value becomes the unmodified Defense for both Pokémon. Unmodified Special Defense is averaged the same way.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Averages Defense and Special Defense with the target.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    The user employs its psychic power to average its Defense and Sp. Def stats with those of its target’s.
