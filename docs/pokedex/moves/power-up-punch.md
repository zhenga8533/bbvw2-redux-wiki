## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Raises the user’s Attack by one stage.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Raises the user’s Attack by one stage after inflicting damage.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    Striking opponents over and over makes the user’s fists harder. Hitting a target raises the Attack stat.
