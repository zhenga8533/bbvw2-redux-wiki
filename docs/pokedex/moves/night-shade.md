## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts damage equal to the user’s level. Type immunity applies, but other type effects are ignored.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Inflicts damage equal to the user’s level.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    The user makes the target see a frightening mirage. It inflicts damage matching the user’s level.
