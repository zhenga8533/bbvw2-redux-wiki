## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. User loses its next turn to "recharge", and cannot attack or switch out during that turn.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    User foregoes its next turn to recharge.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    The user launches a huge boulder at the target to attack. It must rest on the next turn, however.
