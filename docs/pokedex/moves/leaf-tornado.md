## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Has a 50% chance to lower the target’s accuracy by one stage.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Has a 50% chance to lower the target’s accuracy by one stage.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    The user attacks its target by encircling it in sharp leaves. This attack may also lower the target’s accuracy.
