## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. The greater the user’s Speed compared to the target’s, the higher power this move has, to a maximum of 150. User’s Speed | Power -------------------------------- | ----: Up to 2× the target’s Speed | 60 Up to 3× the target’s Speed | 80 Up to 4× the target’s Speed | 120 More than 4× the target’s Speed | 150

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Power is higher when the user has greater Speed than the target, up to a maximum of 150.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    The user hurls an electric orb at the target. The faster the user is than the target, the greater the damage.
