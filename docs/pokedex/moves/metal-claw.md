## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage. Has a 10% chance to raise the user’s Attack one stage.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Has a 10% chance to raise the user’s Attack by one stage.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    The target is raked with steel claws. It may also raise the user’s Attack stat.
