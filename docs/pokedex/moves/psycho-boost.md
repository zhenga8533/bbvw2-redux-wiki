## :material-information: Effect

!!! info "Description"

    Inflicts regular damage, then lowers the user’s Special Attack by two stages.

!!! tip "Quick Summary"

    Lowers the user’s Special Attack by two stages after inflicting damage.

## :material-book-open: In-Game Description

!!! quote "Black 2 & White 2"

    The user attacks the target at full power. The attack’s recoil harshly reduces the user’s Sp. Atk stat.
